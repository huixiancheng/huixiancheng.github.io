Huixian Cheng, Xianfeng Han, Guoqiang Xiao

poster / bibtex

Applying simple existing techniques to 3D semantic segmentation, it may not be very novelty or fancy. However on the SemanticKITTI and SemanticPOSS datasets, it brings FIDNET to the SOTA level based on range images for 3D semantic segmentation.
